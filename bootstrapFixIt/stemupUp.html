What is StemUP?

Discovery Week

A fun week of discovery of science and engineering for High School Juniors who are interested in the STEM fields.

Summer Bridge

A residential program designed to prepare entering TU freshman students for success in the STEM fields.

Scholars Program

The program seeks to nurture a community of undergraduates that collectively grow as students, scholars, researchers, and individuals.

Research Initiation

An academic year program aimed at allowing participants to search for a TU faculty mentor in a STEM field and identifying a research project.

Research Interns

An academic year program offering the opportunity to engage in a research project with a TU faculty mentor working on an STEM field.

Summer Interns

Offers the opportunity to engage full time in a research project with a TU faculty mentor working on an STEM field during the summer.
